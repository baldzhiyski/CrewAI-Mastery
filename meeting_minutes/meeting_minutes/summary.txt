Fintech Plus reported a stellar Q2 2023 with a revenue of $125 million, a 25% increase year-over-year and a gross profit margin of 58%. EBITDA surged to $37.5 million (30% EBITDA margin) while net income rose to $16 million from $10 million in Q2 2022. The total addressable market grew due to the expansion of high yield savings products and asset-backed securities portfolio diversification. Fintech Plus invested $25 million in AAA-rated corporate bonds, total assets reached 1.5 billion with liabilities at 900 million, and a debt-to-equity ratio of 1.5. User growth was substantial with CAC dropping by 15% and LTV growing by 25%. The forecast for Q3 is positive with revenue expected to be around $135 million. An IPO of subsidiary Pay Plus is expected to raise $200 million. Risk management measures are in place.
